# Domain Suspension Policy

## Overview
This policy outlines the circumstances under which domains may be suspended and the procedures for reactivation.

## Section 4.2: Suspension Reasons

Domains may be suspended for the following reasons:

1. **Policy Violations**: Violation of our Terms of Service, Acceptable Use Policy, or any applicable laws and regulations.

2. **Missing WHOIS Information**: Incomplete, inaccurate, or missing WHOIS information that violates ICANN requirements.

3. **Abuse Reports**: Verified reports of spam, phishing, malware distribution, or other malicious activities.

4. **Non-Payment**: Failure to pay for domain registration or renewal fees within the grace period.

5. **Legal Requirements**: Court orders, law enforcement requests, or other legal obligations.

## Section 4.3: Notification Process

When a domain is suspended:
- Email notifications are sent to the registered email address
- Notifications may be delayed in cases of email delivery issues
- Notifications are sent within 24 hours of suspension
- Check your spam folder if you haven't received a notification

## Section 4.4: Reactivation Process

To reactivate a suspended domain, you must:

1. **Update WHOIS Information**: Ensure all WHOIS details are accurate and complete
   - Full legal name or business name
   - Valid email address
   - Correct mailing address
   - Valid phone number

2. **Resolve Policy Violations**: Address any violations that led to suspension
   - Remove prohibited content
   - Comply with Acceptable Use Policy
   - Resolve any legal issues

3. **Contact Support**: Reach out to our abuse team for review
   - Use the abuse contact form on our website
   - Email: abuse@example.com
   - Include your domain name and account information
   - Provide evidence of compliance if applicable

4. **Review Period**: Allow 2-5 business days for review and reactivation

## Section 4.5: Escalation

If your domain suspension is disputed or you believe it was made in error:
- Contact the abuse team with detailed explanation
- Provide evidence supporting your case
- Escalation requests are reviewed within 48 hours
