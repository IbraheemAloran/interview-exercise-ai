# Account Recovery and Password Reset

## Password Reset

### I Forgot My Password

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password" link
3. Enter your email address or username
4. Check your email for password reset instructions
5. Click the reset link (valid for 24 hours)
6. Create a new strong password

**Important**:
- Reset links expire after 24 hours
- You must have access to the email address on your account
- If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder
- Contact support if you don't receive the reset email within 15 minutes

### Creating a Strong Password

Your password must:
- Be at least 12 characters long
- Include uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include at least one number
- Include at least one special character
- Not be a common word or phrase
- Not contain your username or email

## Account Recovery

### I Lost Access to My Email

If you no longer have access to the email address on your account:

1. Contact support immediately
2. Provide proof of account ownership:
   - Government-issued photo ID
   - Previous invoices or receipts
   - Last 4 digits of payment method
   - Account creation date (if known)
   - Domain names associated with account
3. Answer security questions (if set)
4. Allow 2-3 business days for manual verification

### Account Compromised

If you suspect your account has been compromised:

1. **Immediately**: Contact support via phone or urgent ticket
2. Change your password (if you still have access)
3. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
4. Review recent account activity
5. Check for unauthorized changes:
   - Domain transfers
   - Payment method changes
   - Email address changes
   - DNS modifications

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

**Setting Up 2FA**:
1. Go to **Settings > Security**
2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Scan QR code with authenticator app
4. Enter verification code to confirm
5. Save backup codes in a secure location

**Recovery Codes**:
- Generated when you enable 2FA
- Use if you lose access to your authenticator app
- Store in a secure location (password manager)
- Can be regenerated if lost

## Username Recovery

### I Forgot My Username

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Username"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for your username
5. If email not received, contact support

## Account Lockout

### My Account is Locked

Accounts may be locked after multiple failed login attempts:

1. Wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock
2. Use password reset to unlock immediately
3. Contact support if account remains locked
4. Provide account information for verification

## Follow-Up Required

If account recovery requires additional verification:
- Support will contact you via phone or email
- Respond promptly to verification requests
- Provide requested documentation
- Allow 2-5 business days for complete recovery
